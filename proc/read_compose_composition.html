Reads the electron fraction Y_e = n_e/n_b, with n_e electron number density and n_b baryon number density, from the .compo file taken from the \(\mathrm{CompOSE}\) database of EoS. Y_e is given as a function of T, n_b, Y_q on a grid; the computation of Ye on the stars is done by the SUBROUTINE compute_Ye_on_stars.
